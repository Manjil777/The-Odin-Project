Chimichurri Sauce

Prep Time : 5 mins

Cook TIme : 5 mins

Total Time : 12 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • ¾ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot®)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine parsley, oil, vinegar, oregano, cumin, garlic, hot sauce, and salt in
    a blender or food processor. Mix on medium speed until ingredients are evenly
    blended, about 10 seconds.
